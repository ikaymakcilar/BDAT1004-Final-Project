What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency, without a central bank or single administrator, that can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries. It's known for its revolutionary blockchain technology, which ensures security and transparency in financial transactions.
